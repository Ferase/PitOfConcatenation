The True Northeast is my home, and I mean it. No seriously, I live here.

- Meg Lipke

Paris Smeraldo and Meg Lipke opened Northeast Kingdom in the Fall of 2005 in Bushwick, with the help of family and friends. The husband/wife team found inspiration for the restaurant in the farming communities and deer camps of their native Vermont. Our contemporary-American menu emphasizes seasonality and sourcing from local farms and organic purveyors.
